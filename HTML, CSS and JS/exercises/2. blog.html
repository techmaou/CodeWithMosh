My Cozy Coffee

Did you know that coffee beans are not actually beans?
They are seeds found inside the fruit, or "cherries," of the coffee plant.

Coffee is a beloved beverage enjoyed by millions of people around the world. Whether it's the energizing kick to start the day or a comforting companion during a break, coffee holds a special place in our daily routines.
